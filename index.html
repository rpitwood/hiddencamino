Welcome to Hidden Camino

Hidden Camino is a wandering project exploring the forgotten layers of the Camino de Santiago — sacred stones, lost tracks, and the slow rhythm of rural Galician life.

We walk. We talk. We stop and notice. These aren’t tours — they’re quiet conversations with the land, shared between pilgrims and those who stayed behind.

Based near Palas de Rei and Melide, on the Camino Francés. Walks most mornings. Questions welcome.
